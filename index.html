Junbong Jang

M.S. student at KAIST

I am the first-year master's student in the Computer Vision and Learning Lab at KAIST supervised by Dr. Tae-kyun Kim. Before joining KAIST, I worked as a research assistant for Dr. Kwonmoo Lee at Boston Children's Hospital to develop a deep learning-based segmentation pipeline. At Worcester Polytechnic Institute, I worked on "Bioinformatics Approach to Diagnose Mental Disorders" for my thesis project, supervised by Dr. Dmitry Korkin

My research interests are Computer Vision and Healthcare AI.
